Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SWU League Manager ("the Bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot service.

By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Our Commitment: We are committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect data necessary to provide tournament management functionality and never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Bot, we automatically collect the following information:

Data Type Purpose Retention
Discord User ID Identify users and track tournament participation Indefinite or until deletion requested
Discord Username Display player names in tournaments Updated automatically, historical data retained
Server (Guild) ID Organize tournaments by Discord server Indefinite or until league deletion
Channel IDs Send announcements and pairings Stored with league data
Command Interactions Process bot commands and log requests Console logs (temporary)

2.2 User-Provided Information

Information you provide when using the Bot includes:

  • Tournament Data: League names, formats, competition types, round counts
  • Match Results: Wins, losses, draws reported by players
  • Registration Data: Tournament registrations and player participation

2.3 Calculated Information

We calculate and store:

  • Tournament standings and rankings
  • Tiebreaker statistics (OMW%, GW%, OGW%)
  • Match history and pairings
  • Player statistics across tournaments

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Tournament Management: Creating pairings, tracking results, calculating standings
  • Service Operation: Processing commands, sending announcements, managing timers
  • Statistics: Providing player stats, leaderboards, and tournament history
  • Audit Logging: Tracking tournament modifications for transparency
  • Service Improvement: Understanding usage patterns and fixing bugs

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Method

Your data is stored in a secure database using industry-standard practices:

  • SQLite database with regular backups
  • Stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • No credit card or payment information is collected
  • No passwords or authentication tokens are stored

4.2 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Limited access to data storage systems
  • Regular security updates and maintenance
Important: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Active Tournaments: Data retained while tournaments are in progress
  • Completed Tournaments: Historical data retained indefinitely for statistics and history features
  • Deleted Leagues: Tournament data deleted, audit logs preserved
  • Console Logs: Temporary, typically overwritten within days/weeks

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Use your data for purposes other than tournament management
  • Transfer data outside of the Bot's operational infrastructure

We MAY disclose your information:

  • Within Discord servers where you participate in tournaments (usernames, results, standings)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Bot, its users, or the public

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

7.1 Right to Access

You can view your tournament history, match results, and statistics using the Bot's commands (/stats player, /history).

7.2 Right to Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting the bot administrator. Please note:

  • Historical tournament data may be anonymized rather than deleted to preserve tournament integrity
  • Some data may need to be retained for audit and transparency purposes
  • Complete removal may take up to 30 days

7.3 Right to Correction

Your Discord username is automatically updated. Match results can be corrected by tournament creators using the /tournament modifymatch command.

7.4 Right to Object

You can stop using the Bot at any time. Simply don't register for tournaments or use Bot commands.

8. Children's Privacy

The Bot does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can remove the data.

Users must comply with Discord's age requirements (13+ or 16+ depending on location).

9. Discord Integration

This Bot operates on Discord and is subject to Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing:

  • Discord Terms of Service
  • Discord Privacy Policy

We only access Discord information necessary for Bot functionality through Discord's official API.

10. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies. All interactions occur through Discord's interface.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will attempt to notify users through Discord announcements where possible.

12. International Users

The Bot may be used by individuals from various countries. By using the Bot, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of your location.

13. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may affect your personal information, we will:

  • Investigate the breach promptly
  • Take steps to mitigate harm
  • Notify affected users through Discord where possible
  • Comply with applicable data breach notification laws

14. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact:

  • Discord: Contact the bot administrator through Discord
  • GitHub: github.com/krystlih/swu-league-manager

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

15. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Consent: By using the Bot, you consent to data collection and processing
  • Legitimate Interest: Providing tournament management services
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary to provide the service you requested

By using SWU League Manager, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
